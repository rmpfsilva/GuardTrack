🔧 PHASE 1 – Live Fill Visibility (No Schema Changes)

Enhance UI only.

From existing JSONB fields:

Compute:

totalRequired = sum(positions.count)
totalAccepted = sum(acceptedPositions.count)
remaining = totalRequired - totalAccepted
fillPercentage = totalAccepted / totalRequired

Add to job share cards:

Progress bar

“X of Y filled”

Remaining number

Colour indicator:

Green = 100%

Amber = 50–99%

Red = <50%

No database changes required.
